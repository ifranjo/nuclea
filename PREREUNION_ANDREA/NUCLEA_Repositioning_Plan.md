## Strategic Shift to AI-Ethics Leadership

**Current Situation:**
- Death tech market has significant AI controversy (Everlived, etc.)
- NUCLEA's 5-capsule system naturally avoids AI but hasn't leveraged this
- Competitors face regulatory and reputational risks

**New Positioning:**
> "NUCLEA: Human Legacy for the AI Age"
> The ethical alternative in digital legacy.

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### New Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Includes | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | 1 capsule, 5GB | Trial users |
| **Personal** | $9.99/mo | 5 capsules, 50GB | Individual |
| **Family** | $19.99/mo | Unlimited, 500GB, multi-user | Families |
| **Legacy** | $49.99/mo | All + API + White-label | Power users/B2B |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Funeral homes, insurers | B2B |

### Value Proposition

```
Free vs Personal:
- Limited to 1 capsule prevents full experience
- 5GB storage fills quickly
- Upgrade friction is intentional

Personal vs Family:
- Family pricing is 2x but supports 4 users
- Multi-user is key differentiator
- "Share the legacy, not the subscription"

Family vs Legacy:
- Legacy includes API access
- White-label for funeral partners
- Revenue share opportunity
```
